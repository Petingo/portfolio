About

I am Yang Hong-Bin, aka Petingo, from Taiwan.

Currently, I work as a research assistant @ Academia Sinica, Taiwan, conducting research related to automatic image synthesis and image forgery detection under the supervision of Prof. Chen, Jun-Cheng.

Before starting my job, I have completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science @ Taiwan Tech, and exchanged to Czech Technical University in Prague during the harsh time of COVID-19. My previous research focused on image quality enhancement with both mathematical methods and deep learning, and I had cooperated with Prof. Kuo-Liang Chung and published Effective Chroma Subsampling and Luma Modification for RGB Full-color Images Using the Multiple Linear Regression Technique, which is the state-of-the-art of YUV420 chroma subsampling (Dec. 2020).
